About the Customer Feedback Analysis Tool

Our Customer Feedback Analysis Tool is designed to help retailers understand their customers' sentiments and identify common issues and areas for improvement. By analyzing feedback from various sources such as reviews, social media, and surveys, our tool provides valuable insights that can enhance product and service quality.

How to Use the Tool

  1. Navigate to the Feedback Analysis tab.
  2. Upload a CSV file containing customer feedback with columns such as "feedback", "review", or "text".
  3. Click the "Analyze" button to generate sentiment analysis, word clouds, and other visual insights.
  4. Review the generated insights and recommendations to help improve your products and services.
